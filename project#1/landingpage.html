About Us

What we do is more than an online grocery store! our mission is to deliver 100% accurate and unbiased facts about food, nutrition, and health and at the same time, deliver fresh produce from our local farmers straight to your doorsteps. Allowing city folk access to fresher produce and real farm-to-table experiences in the comforts of home.We collaborate with local farmers who struggle to get their fresh produce to the Metro safely, damaging the quality of the fruits and vegetables by carefully picking the produce ourselves, while handling distribution and logistics via our refrigerated trucks.
